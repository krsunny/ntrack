Features

Warehousing and distribution

Track the stocks in each of your warehouse and decide for restocking based on predictive analysis.

Order Management

Track your invoices generated at real time keeping track of product sales.

Inventory Control

Supervises the flow of goods from manufacturers to warehouses and from these facilities to point of sale.

Sales reports and Business Intelligence

Generates real time sales reports and applies predictive analysis to provide insights on sales happening throughout your network.

Supplier Management

Maintain a record of your suppliers and manufacturers and know exactly how many days before you need to raise a new request with them to meet demand.

Multiple Channel Integration

Expand your business by integrating your amazon, ebay, flipcart accounts and spike up your sales. This feature coupled with our ability to forecast demands can lead your business to new grounds.
